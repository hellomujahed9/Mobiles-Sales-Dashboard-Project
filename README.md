# Mobiles-Sales-Dashboard-Project


​📌 Project Overview
​This project is an interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard designed to track and analyze mobile phone sales performance. The dashboard provides a comprehensive 360-degree view of business metrics, helping stakeholders understand sales trends, brand performance, and customer behavior across different regions in India.
​📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)
​Total Sales: ₹76.92 Crores (Total Revenue generated).
​Total Quantity: 19,150+ units sold.
​Transactions: 3,835+ successful orders.
​Average Price: ₹40,000 per unit (Average selling price).
​🚀 Key Features & Visualizations
​Geographical Analysis: An interactive India Map showing sales distribution by major cities like New Delhi, Hyderabad, and Mumbai.
​Time-Series Trends: A Line Chart displaying monthly sales fluctuations, highlighting peaks in July and November.
​Brand Performance: A detailed Table & Bar Chart comparing top brands like Apple, Samsung, OnePlus, Vivo, and Xiaomi.
​Customer Feedback: A Rating Status bar chart categorizing mobile performance into Good, Average, and Poor.
​Payment Insights: A Pie Chart showing the breakdown of transactions by payment methods (UPI, Debit Card, Credit Card).
​Day-wise Analysis: An area chart analyzing sales performance by days of the week (Monday - Sunday).
​🛠️ Technical Stack
​Tool: Microsoft Excel / Power BI (Specify which one you used).
​Data Processing: Power Query for data cleaning and transformation.
​Formulas: Used DAX (if Power BI) or Pivot Tables/VLOOKUP (if Excel) for complex calculations.
​Interactivity: Integrated Slicers for Mobile Model, Payment Method, and Brand filtering.
​📈 Business Insights
​Product Leader: The iPhone SE contributed the highest revenue (~60M) among all models.
​Payment Preferences: Customer preference is evenly distributed across UPI, Debit, and Credit cards (approx. 24-26% each).
​Seasonal Spikes: Significant sales growth was observed during the mid-year (July) and year-end (November) periods.
